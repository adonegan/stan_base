Stanbase!

Have you ever loved something so much that you want to write all about it? Do you follow the careers of your favourite stars? Well, the nature of fandom is where this app comes from. This website and app is where you get to keep a record of the stuff you love... about Winona Ryder. Read about her films and see what other stans think. Any stan can read, edit, update or even remove the records! This is your place, your Stanbase.

Winona Ryder

Winona Ryder is an American actress, known currently for her role as Joyce Byers in Netflix's hit science-fiction-horror TV show Stranger Things.Having started acting in 1986, you'll most likely know her for her roles in late eighties/early nineties cult classics like Heathers, Beetlejuice, Dracula and Reality Bites. This Stanbase! keeps a record of all of them!

" I went from weirdo teenager to pixie waif to them not knowing what the hell to do with me. "
- Winona Ryder
What is a stan?

If you were a teenager in the year 2000, you might remember Eminem's classic hit song and music video Stan, featuring British singer Dido. "My tea's gone cold, I'm wondering why I got out of bed at all..." - the song below inspired this app. Nearly 20 years on from the chilling video, to stan someone nowadays is less Stanley 'Stan' Mitchell and more superfan. This app is dedicated to the modern and completely harmless stan.

If you've come this far, having signed up and logged in, you are a stan! So, welcome. If you don't see a record on a Winona Ryder film in the list, add it! If you think something more could be said about a film already on the list, update it! This is the place Winona stans of the world unite!
